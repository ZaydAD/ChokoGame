The Game

Choko is a 2 player game that focuses on strategy, speed and competitiveness.

The goal of the game is to capture all of your opponent's pieces!

May the best player win!

Rules

  • At the start, each player receives 12 pawns
  • Each player can move one square at a time in all directions (except diagonally)
  • To capture a pawn, a player must jump over the opponent's pawn with their own
  • The first to capture all of the opponent's pieces wins!
